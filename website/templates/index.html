Who are we?

Mustard Seed Ministry is an on-campus Christian ministry with the purpose of being and making disciples of Christ. We provide many avenues to grow in faith and mature in our relationship with God, such as discipleship training and tight-knit small groups. Our hope for those in our ministry is to be a people who journey to know Jesus more and to make Him known.
